## Writing strategy

- Core focus: YOLO detection, lane segmentation, 3D spatial estimation, rule reasoning, system implementation
- Secondary focus: plate recognition and video tracking as engineering enhancement modules
- Strong engineering point: one integrated system that supports image and video analysis, interactive configuration, and result persistence
- Strong algorithm point: multi-source fusion rather than single-model classification
